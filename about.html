About This Project

My name is Wout Neutkens. I am an independent thinker, interested in the foundations of reality: from computational physics, theories of consciousness (IIT), and finitistic mathematics to the moral question of how we can eliminate suffering and stack happiness.

The essay, The Cathedral of Cause and Experience, is the first stone. It postulates a single, reliable rule as the foundation of everything. From that follows a path toward sufficient safety and an infinite stacking of happiness — not as a utopia, but as a technical and moral project.

On the Blog, reflections, additions, and insights appear regularly.
