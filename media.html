Media

The cathedral is not made of stone and text alone. Sometimes there is speaking, drawing, listening. Here I collect informal creations that accompany the essay and the blog.

Readings

The Battle for Certainty

Based on the blogpost "The Battle for Certainty". We can achieve near-certainty through converging evidence, absolute certainty is unattainable because all models rest on unprovable assumptions, so the most responsible stance is an open attitude that values a model for its consistent explanatory power without claiming it as the sole reality.

Infinite Happiness

The core message of the reading "Infinite Happiness" is inspired by the essay. We live in a universe that is fundamentally reliable, and reliability offers us the possibility to build an infinite cathedral of experience.

The Universal Rule

This reading introduces the Universal Rule as a dynamic alternative to static, infinite mathematics. The ultimate goal is a practical vision of the future, in which humanity uses these reliable foundations to transcend suffering and consciously build a state of sustainable happiness.
